Company Journey

Our Growth Story

🚀
2018 — FOUNDING
Kahyata's Consultancy Firm is Founded

Founded in Lusaka, Zambia by Kahyata Muzaza with a vision to bring world-class technology consulting to African businesses and beyond — agile, intelligent, and results-driven.

📈
2020 — GROWTH
First Enterprise Contract

Secured a landmark systems engineering contract, delivering scalable network infrastructure and custom software solutions that demonstrated the firm's technical depth and reliability.

Dedicated AI/ML practice established to help clients integrate intelligent automation, predictive analytics, and LLM-powered tools into their operations.
